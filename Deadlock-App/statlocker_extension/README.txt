1. Open Google Chrome browser.
2. In the address bar, type chrome://extensions/ and press Enter.
3. In the top right corner, toggle the Developer mode switch to ON.
4. Click the Load unpacked button that appears in the top left corner.
5. In the file selection dialog, select the deadlock_extension folder you prepared in Step 1.
6. Verify that the Deadlock Draft Bridge card appears in the list and the toggle switch on the card is enabled.
